Hello, my name is Hung Dinh Xuan. I am a Master student at the Department of Information and Telecommunication Engineering, Soongsil University, Seoul, South Korea. I received my BEng. degree in Information Systems from the University of Information Technology, Vietnam National University, Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. My research interests include deep learning, machine learning, and audio deepfake detection. I am particularly interested in developing algorithms for detect audio generated by AI. I am also interested in applying machine learning techniques to solve real-world problems. If you are interested in working with me, please feel free to contact me.
